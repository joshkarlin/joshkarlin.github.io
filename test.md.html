Markdeep Feature Demo** Morgan McGuire This demonstration documents the features of [Markdeep](http://casual-effects.com/markdeep) and acts as a test for it. Markdeep is a text formatting syntax that extends Markdown, and a Javascript program for making it work in browsers. The two most powerful features are its ability to run in any **web browser** on the client side and the inclusion of **diagrams**. [Click here](https://casual-effects.com/markdeep/features.md.html?noformat) to see this document without automatic formatting. Markdeep is free and easy to use. It doesn't need a plugin, or Internet connection. There's nothing to install. Just start writing in Vi, Nodepad, Zed, Emacs, Visual Studio, Atom, or another editor! You don't have to export, compile, or otherwise process your document. If you want to support development of Markdeep, just buy my [Graphics Codex](http://graphicscodex.com) book for $10 on Amazon. Revenue from that funds my open source projects.
